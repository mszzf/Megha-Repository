E-SMART

Smart Solutions to save energy

Home comfort application senses a user's
home temperature,relative humdity and
based on that generates a comfort index.
User can then change the settings
to get the best comfort index.The tool
also generates recommendations to
achieve the best comfort by most
economic means.
Energy Analyser tool analyses the user's
energy utilization and generates reports
and recommendations on how to cut down
one's utility bill.The tool provides detailed
analysis which can be narrowed down to
and also monthly analysis along with weather
conditions and utility cost.
Based on the money saved by the user
on his utility bills in comparison to
other user's in his community the tool
computes reward points for the user.
The user can then use the reward points
to play games on facebook.The tool
also picks household of the month and
each user on the community can see
other users reward points.
With find my neighborhood the user can find
a neighborhood based on his requirements.
The tool aids in the search by providing user
with neighborhood's average annul utility
cost,maintenance cost, rent etc.It also
provides directions and supports navigation
to the selected community.
With discussion board the user can create
threads to discuss their utility bill problems
with other users.They can also share tips
and recommendations on how to save
on utility and home comfort.
